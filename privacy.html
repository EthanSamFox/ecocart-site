Privacy Policy – EcoCart

Last updated: 14 Sep 2025

EcoCart lets merchants offer an optional “carbon neutral order” toggle and display a badge.

Information we process

  • Shop data: shop domain and app settings (offset variant ID, display text), and counts of offset usage.
  • Order metadata (optional): line-item info used only to count how many orders included the offset. We do not store customer personal data.

How we use information

  • Provide and improve the EcoCart app.
  • Show basic statistics to the merchant.

Sharing

We do not sell or rent data. Limited data may be shared if required by law.

Retention & deletion

We retain shop settings while the app is installed. Upon uninstall or written request, we delete shop data within 30 days.
