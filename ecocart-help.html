EcoCart — Quick setup (2 minutes)

  1. Create (or use) a product named Carbon Offset (e.g., £0.50) and publish it to Online Store. Open the variant and copy the number at the end of its URL (…/variants/1234567890) — that’s your Variant ID.
  2. Theme Editor → Product template → Add block → EcoCart Toggle → paste your Variant ID → Save. (Optionally add the EcoCart Badge.)

Troubleshooting

  • Blocks don’t appear? Refresh the Theme Editor and ensure your theme is Online Store 2.0 (e.g., Dawn).
  • Toggling does nothing? Ensure the Carbon Offset product is published and the Variant ID is correct.
